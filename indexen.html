Resume

Resume

Sissi Fleitas is a renowned Cuban-American personality followed by hundreds of thousands on Instagram. Her career began on stage at age four and took off on TV at fifteen with Cubavisión and later Univisión’s “Sábado Gigante.” As an actress, host and communicator, she’s starred in hit telenovelas, anchored the Spanish version of “The Tonight Show,” and appeared at major international festivals. She made her film debut in 2010 and starred in the Cannes-selected feature “Rumble.” A cover girl for magazines like Maxim and People en Español, she founded the beauty line ElyssBel by Sissi Fleitas, has developed real estate ventures, serves as a global brand ambassador and actively supports philanthropic initiatives across Latin America and the U.S.

Real Estate

Philanthropy

Philanthropy

Sissi Fleitas combines her artistic career with a strong social commitment, collaborating with various organizations. She is a board member of Casita Maria in the Bronx, promoting art and education for youth. She is an Ambassador of the Florida Grand Opera, promoting access to opera among Hispanic youth. In Miami, she supports Supreme Twins Foundation, focused on opportunities for at-risk youth, and is part of the Advisory Board of MISO, promoting symphonic music. She also collaborates with Goodwill South Florida, supporting labor inclusion for people with disabilities. Through her digital presence and participation in events, Sissi inspires with messages of empowerment and social transformation.
